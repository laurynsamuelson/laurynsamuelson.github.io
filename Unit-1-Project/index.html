My Favorite Avocado Toast Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Egg whites carton
  • 2 slices of sour dough bread
  • 1 Avocado
  • crumbled feta cheese
  • spicy honey
  • salt
  • red pepper flakes
  • Everything but the bagel seasoning
  • 1 lemon

I get a lot of these ingredients from trader joe's. Trader Joe's website


If you don't have sour dough bread, you can use any kind of bread you want.

Steps:

  1. Crack 2 eggs into a pan and fry them at medium heat. Try not to break the yolk up.
  2. Pour the egg whites into the pan until it fills the bottom.
  3. Put butter in pan and toast the sour dough bread on the stove.
  4. Carve out the avocado mash.
  5. Squeeze a lemon on top of the avocado and mix together.
  6. Add salt, red pepper flakes, and everything but the bagel seasoning. Mix together.
  7. Spread avocado on toast. Then, add the fried eggs on top.
  8. Sprinkle feta cheese on top. Then glaze it with spicy honey.

Then, eat a perfect avocado toast with a fun beverage. Enjoy!
