Math Symbols

The > sign shows when a number has higher value than another number

The < sign shows when a number has lower value than another number.

  • Examples:
    1. 7 > 4
    2. 3 < 5
    3. 6 > 2

You can even use these inequalities to solve equations.

  • Examples:
    1. x + 3 > 2
    2. 7x < 28
    3. 2y + 1 < 7
